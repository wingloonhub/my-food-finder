      How to paste a Google Maps link
      1. In Google Maps, search the restaurant and tap it to open its page.
      2. Tap the Share button (not Directions) → Copy link.
      3. Come back here, paste it in the box above, and tap Go.

      It fills in the name, location and address automatically. The link should start with maps.app.goo.gl — a share.google link won’t carry the location.

      Searching in Malaysia. Paste a Google Maps link to auto-fill, or type a name to look it up — if it isn’t found, just add it by hand.
